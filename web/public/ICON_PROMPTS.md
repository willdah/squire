# Squire Icon & Logo — Nano Banana 2 Prompts

Generate each variant separately in Gemini (Nano Banana 2) or Google AI Studio.
Generate Variant 4 first, then use it as a reference input image for the others
to keep character consistency.

Nano Banana doesn't support transparent backgrounds — use a solid background and
remove it in post with a background removal tool.

## Variant 1 — App Icon (favicon, PWA, mobile)

A flat vector app icon of a friendly robot head wearing a medieval knight helmet, viewed straight-on. The robot has a round face, glowing cyan eyes (#67e8f9), and a subtle smile. The helmet and body are cool steel gray (#94a3b8). The background is a solid deep indigo-navy circle (#1e1b4b). Clean flat illustration style with soft highlights on the helmet — no gradients on the face, no text, no border, no drop shadow. The design is simple enough to read clearly at very small sizes like a browser favicon. Square composition, centered, with generous padding. 1:1 aspect ratio.

Export at 512px, then downscale to 192, 180 (apple-touch), 48, 32, 16.

## Variant 2 — Sidebar Logomark

A minimal flat vector icon of a friendly robot head in three-quarter view, facing right. It wears a small medieval knight helmet with a visor. One glowing cyan eye (#67e8f9) is visible. The helmet and head are steel gray. White background, no other elements, no text. Extremely simple — only the essential shapes so it reads clearly at 24 by 24 pixels. Clean flat illustration, minimal detail. 1:1 aspect ratio.
